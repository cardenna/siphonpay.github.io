How it works

Create a group in our web app and pay into it with your friends. When ready, we will generate a digital Visa card with your group's total as the limit. The card can be used for paying online.

Refunding

Change of plans? No worries, we guarantee a full refund of your payments as long as you have not spent any money from the generated card.
